Roses Are LED



A Rose, to shine when the world seems Dark

A piece I made in the darkness of winter to expand my understanding of phosphorescent pigment. The Idea is that Transparent phosphorescent petals surround a UV LED which is powered by solar panels in the same way as garden path lights. The Daylight charges the petals, which glow softly until full darkness, when the LED lights up, charging the petals up again from the center of the rose.
