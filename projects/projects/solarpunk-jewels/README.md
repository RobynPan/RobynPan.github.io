# Jewelry Made of PCBs

tags: PCB, Jewellery, Electronics, Wearable, Solarpunk, Fabrication, HAEQS

## The Jewels of our Age

Printed circuit boards are the substrate of the modern world — etched copper on fibreglass, carrying signals we depend on but rarely see. I wanted to make them visible. Wearable. Precious.

These pieces treat PCBs as the raw material for jewellery: cut, shaped, layered, and combined with traditional findings to sit around a neck or hang from an ear. The green solder mask. The gold fingers. The silkscreen traces. All of it normally hidden inside a device — here worn on the outside.

The project came out of HAEQS and a long conversation about what "natural materials" means in 2017. Stone, bone, wood — or silicon, copper, resin? The jewels of our age are these.
