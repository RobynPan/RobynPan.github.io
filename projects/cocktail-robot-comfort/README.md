# Cocktail Robot Comfort

tags: Robotics, Art, Performance, Interaction, Cocktails

video: projects/cocktail-robot-comfort/images/MakeMeAComfort.mp4

In December 2025 I joined the Roboexotica (https://roboexotica.at/), possibly the longest running cocktail-robot event in the world, with my robot "Comfort" which dispensed a comforting Margarita to visitors. 
I was given the award for 'Best Serving' cocktail robot.

I really enjoyed how varied the process was: The Scan of my face was fixed up in Blender, then adapted with tubing mounts in Fusion360. The Microcontroller controlled the peristaltic pumps using the optocoupler SV-5A1R1P-N which which I suspect will quickly become my go-to for future projects. Interface for activating the robot was a CNC'd Bronze Plaque with "Comfort" on it, which i hooked up as a capacitive touch sensor. Finally, i spent a day in the wood workshop cutting, planing, sanding, painting and finally oiling the wood for the stand. It's good to have the full resources of a makerspace like hashtag#Motionlab at your back, even for small and fun projects! 😁

The installation of the robot was accompanied by following text: 

In interesting times, the world can feel like a harsh, disheartening place. Eyes open or not, we can all occasionally do with some Comfort. This comfort comes, as it sometimes does, in the form of a Margarita: Tequila, Triple Sec and Lime juice. Bitter, Sour, Sweet - and comforting.
