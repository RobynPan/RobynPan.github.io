Hi! I'm Robyn; I'm somewhere between a hacker and maker. I'm a cofounder of HAEQS, a Berlin based Queer*-Feminist Hack-Collective, and I'm a student of Humanoid Robotics at The Beuth University of Applied Sciences Berlin. I'm also a member of the Edinburgh Hacklab, and was a regular face at the Metalab in Vienna before moving to Berlin in 2015.

Whether in Robotics, Wearables or Art projects, I center my projects on the fluid connection between the familiar and the new, or unfamiliar.
